Alisha Mercier

Bio

Alisha Mercier is a California native who has dreamed of being a published author her whole life. She earned her bachelor's degree from CalState Dominguez Hills and is currently an MFA canidate at Antioch University's Culver City, CA campus. Her first novel Wannabe is a Y/A comming of age set in the 80's. It is the story of Brook Kinsey, an over weight bi-racial teen with a dysfunctional family who can see ghosts.
